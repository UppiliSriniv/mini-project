Achievements



Mahesh Babu has achieved significant milestones throughout his career as a leading actor in Telugu cinema. Here's an overview of his achievements:

1. Awards and Honors

  • Nandi Awards: Mahesh has won several prestigious Nandi Awards, including Best Actor for films like Arjun (2004), Athadu (2005), Dookudu (2011), and Seethamma Vakitlo Sirimalle Chettu (2013).
  • Filmfare Awards South: He has received multiple Filmfare Awards for Best Actor, including for Okkadu (2003), Pokiri (2006), Dookudu (2011), and Srimanthudu (2015).
  • SIIMA Awards: He has been honored with South Indian International Movie Awards (SIIMA) for his outstanding performances.
  • IIFA Utsavam Awards: Recognized for his roles in films like Srimanthudu.


2. Box Office Success

Mahesh Babu has delivered several blockbusters that have set new benchmarks in the Telugu film industry. Films like Pokiri, Dookudu, Srimanthudu, Bharat Ane Nenu (2018), and Sarileru Neekevvaru (2020) have been both critical and commercial successes. Pokiri (2006) was remade in multiple languages and became a turning point in his career. Srimanthudu (2015) and Bharat Ane Nenu highlighted socially relevant themes and became box office hits.

3. Philanthropy and Social Contributions

Mahesh adopted two villages: Burripalem in Andhra Pradesh and Siddhapuram in Telangana. His contributions towards the development of these villages include improving infrastructure, healthcare, and education facilities. He actively supports charities, including working with the Heal-a-Child Foundation, which provides financial support for children in need of medical care.

4. Production and Business Ventures

He founded his production company, G. Mahesh Babu Entertainment Pvt. Ltd., producing films like Srimanthudu and Major (2022), which have received critical acclaim. Mahesh is one of the highest-paid brand ambassadors in South India, endorsing top brands like Thums Up, Idea Cellular, and Santoor.

5. Recognition in Popular Media

Frequently listed in Forbes India's Celebrity 100 list, Mahesh Babu is recognized as one of the most influential celebrities in India. In 2021, he was awarded the Sakshi Excellence Award for his contributions to cinema. These achievements highlight Mahesh Babu's impact both on-screen and off-screen, cementing his status as one of the most admired and successful actors in Indian cinema.

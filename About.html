Early Life and Family

Mahesh Babu was born to veteran Telugu actor Krishna Ghattamaneni and Indira Devi. His family has deep roots in the South Indian film industry, with his father being a prominent actor. He is married to Namrata Shirodkar, a former actress and Miss India, whom he met on the set of the movie Vamsi. The couple has two children, a son named Gautam Krishna and a daughter named Sitara.





Acting Career

Mahesh made his acting debut as a child artist in his father's films, beginning with Needa (1979) at the age of four. His first lead role as an adult came with Rajakumarudu (1999), a film that earned him the Nandi Award for Best Male Debut. He rose to superstardom with movies like Okkadu (2003), Athadu (2005), Pokiri (2006), Dookudu (2011), Seethamma Vakitlo Sirimalle Chettu (2013), and Srimanthudu (2015). His film Pokiri became a landmark in Tollywood and established him as a mass hero.


Acting Style and Appeal

Known for his charm, calm demeanor, and often intense roles, Mahesh is admired for his natural acting, especially in action and drama genres. His on-screen presence and ability to play both mass and class roles have contributed to his versatility. He's often praised for his dialogue delivery, expressions, and subtle acting style, which set him apart in the industry.

Philanthropy and Business

Mahesh is known for his philanthropic work and has adopted villages in Andhra Pradesh and Telangana to develop their infrastructure and education facilities. He is also involved in several business ventures, including his own production company, G. Mahesh Babu Entertainment Pvt. Ltd., through which he has produced several of his own films. Additionally, he is a brand ambassador for numerous big brands, making him one of the highest-paid celebrity endorsers in South India.
